1.  Appointments are required to view apartments.
2. Good Credit is Required - To qualify, each application will be reviewed on the basis of credit, verifiable income, and rental history.
3. Gross monthly combined income for the entire household must equal at least 3 times the monthly rent for the unit the applicants are applying for.
4. No Co-Signors are accepted.
5. All income must be verified – proof can be in the form of two current paycheck stubs, income tax records or bank statements.
6. Applications are required for everyone 18 years of age or older, and must be filled out completely, signed and dated.
7. A photo I.D. must be shown for each applicant.
8. A refundable Security Deposit of $600.00 for Kirkwood I apartments or $900.00 for Kirkwood II apartments must be paid by cashiers check or money order to hold apartment. Cash is not accepted.
9. Rental Agreements are on a month-to-month basis. Residents must give a written 30 day notice to vacate.
10. Rent is pro-rated from move-in-date.
11. No Pets are allowed.
